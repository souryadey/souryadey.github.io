Bio

Hello, welcome to my webpage. I am a PhD student researching on deep learning at the University of Southern California. I am expecting to graduate in May 2020, and am looking for postdoctoral opportunities beyond that. This is the point in my bio where I realize that it's very conventional, without a trace of originality. So, here are the answers to two questions which I am always asking myself:

Is pursuing a PhD really worth it?

Like so many other big questions in life, the correct answer would be It depends. If I were to threshold the answer and make it Yes/No, I would say Yes. A PhD is like getting a regular industry job, except that it pays less and gives you more freedom. And it lets you be a student, which, to me, feels like a nice slap in the face to I'm a grownup professional now. If you want to read a bit more of my thoughts on this matter, go read this and then this.
